Before we get started heres a couple quick instructions

Locations Panel


On the left is the locations panel, this lists off all locations of interest these are sorted by exposure dates.
to filter these click on the "Show/Hide Options" Button and click on the groups you want to hide


Map


On the right is a map of all current locations of interest, This also has a case counter in the top right corner.
If at any time you manage to get lost in the map just click/tap "Reset Map"
